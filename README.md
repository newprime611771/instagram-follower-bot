# Instagram Follower Management with MadHub

MadHub's powerful automation features enable you to effectively manage your Instagram account, enhancing your online presence and engagement through sophisticated tools designed for optimal social media management. With capabilities like **Instagram Account Warm-Up** and **Instagram User Search**, you can streamline your operations, nurture your audience, and significantly improve your account's trust and visibility.

## Key Features

### Instagram Account Warm-Up
MadHub's **Instagram Account Warm-Up** feature is engineered to simulate genuine user interactions by browsing feeds and engaging with content in a way that mimics organic activity. This feature intelligently toggles between homepage and video pages while performing likes and comments as configured. By setting interaction probabilities and execution distributions, you can:
- Enhance account activity and weight
- Minimize the risk of account bans
- Establish trustworthiness, essential for new accounts or those undergoing maintenance

#### Usage
To utilize the **Instagram Account Warm-Up** feature effectively:
1. Navigate to the **Warm-Up** section of the MadHub interface.
2. Set the interaction probability to define how often likes or comments will be performed.
3. Configure the execution distribution to control the frequency of actions across various content types.

#### Configuration Example
- **Interaction Probability**: Configure a probability of 70%, meaning that 7 out of 10 times, your actions will register as genuine user interactions.
- **Execution Distribution**: Set a balanced distribution that allows for varied interactions, ensuring that some actions are prioritized over others, like giving more attention to video content.
